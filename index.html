Details

Founder's Authority

Founder can hire as well as fire professors. He can also view professors' information in details. He can edit his profile and change password.

Founder can create, update and delete his Course Category.

Founder can create, update and delete his Courses but he doesn't have authority to update and delete other professors' courses.

Founder can manage all his categories and courses as well as create courses' curriculum. He can also check students' assignments.

Founder can view all students' as well as his students' data.

Founder can talk to his students via discussion forem. He can also clear all messages.

Professors' Authority

Professor can view other professors' information in details. He can edit his profile and change password. He can leave Education Platform.

Professor can create, update and delete his Course Category but he doesn't have authority to update and delete other professors' categories.

Professor can create, update and delete his Courses but he doesn't have authority to update and delete other professors' courses.

Professor can manage all his categories and courses as well as create courses' curriculum. He can also check students' assignments.

Professor can view all students' as well as his students' data.

Professor can talk to his students via discussion forem. He can also clear all messages.

Students' Authority

Student can view his profile. He can also enroll courses checking all the information he might need.

Student can edit his profile as well as change password.

Student can view his current study courses and study courses. He can rate courses and submit assignment under submission rules.

After passing all Assignment, student will achieve his completed course certification.

Student can talk to his professor and other students.
